Instant Coffee Recipe

How to Brew Instant Coffee at Home Like a Boss

Now, you might think that after all these years you’ve nailed how to make a damn good cuppa. But there are a few small things you can do to make your coffee even more delicious. Below are some tips for brewing a kick-ass instant coffee.

You'll need:

  • We Are Little’s flavoured instant coffee of your choice
  • Your favourite mug
  • Teaspoon
  • Water
  • Kettle
  • Milk/cream (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)

Method:

  1. Boil the kettle (remember to boil a little more than you need)
  2. Pre-heat your mug by filling it with hot water from the kettle. When the cup feels warm, discard the water
  3. Add 2g of Little’s flavoured instant coffee to the mug (approx. 1 heaped teaspoon)
  4. One minute after the kettle has boiled, pour 200ml of hot water over the coffee granules
  5. Stir until the granules dissolve
  6. Add milk, cream or sugar to taste
  7. Serve and enjoy!
